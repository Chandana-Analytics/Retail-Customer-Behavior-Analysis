\# Project Overview



\- This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using online retail transaction data. The goal was to understand how customers shop by looking at how often they place orders, how much they spend, how many products they purchase, and how recently they have interacted with the business.



\- The project started with profiling and cleaning the dataset to identify records that could affect the analysis, such as cancelled transactions, missing customer IDs, and invalid values. After preparing the data, customer-level analyses were performed to uncover purchasing patterns and differences in customer activity.



\- The analysis provides a clearer understanding of customer purchasing behavior by identifying purchasing patterns, customer value, customer activity, and different customer segments. The findings generated from this project support data-driven decision-making and help businesses better understand their customer base.
